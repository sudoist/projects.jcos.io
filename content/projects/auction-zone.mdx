## Summary

A project for making a proposal to updating client's legacy site.

I have been working on maintenance to an auction site's old system.

- Updated Authorize.net codes
- Updated bidding increments
- Fixed minor bugs

Improvements in this project when applying the recommendations.

- New CMS for easy management
    - Adding auctions and items
    - Editing auctions and items
    - Auction display controls
- Improved performance
- Real time updating of bids by polls
