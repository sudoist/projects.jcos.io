## Summary

Restaurant ordering when customers are seated they will be provided with a QR or the table has the QR for them to scan.

The idea is to replicate restaurants in Singapore where when customers are seated they will be provided with a QR or the table has the QR for them to scan.

From previous experiences in the Philippines, the tables have QR codes for customers but it will only download the menu as pdf or provide a link to their site.

- UI framework for this project is [Next.js/React](https://nextjs.org)
- CSS framework with [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com)
- UI deployed on [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/)
- API deployed on [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com/)
